Overnight Oats Recipe

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Peanuts or Peanut Butter
  • Chia Seeds
  • Banana
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Peel Banana and get all things ready
  2. Put all ingredients into a bowl and mixing container
  3. Mix all ingredients in well and place into the fridge for a couple hours or overnight
  4. When ready to eat, mix a little more, add fresh fruit and enjoy!
