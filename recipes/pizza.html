Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Toppings

Instructions

  1. Stretch out dough
  2. Put all ingredients onto the stretch dough including sauce, cheese, and toppings
  3. PLace it intp the oven until the crust is golden brown and cheese is melted
  4. Take it out of the oven, cut it into triangles or squares and enjoy!
