Breakfast Eggs Recipe

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Toast
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Fry the egg scrabbled, sunnyside up, or etc.
  2. Add cheese and salt and pepper to your liking while it is still hot
  3. Place bread into a toaster and take it out when it is perfectly crisp
  4. Pour yourself a glass of millk and enjoy!
